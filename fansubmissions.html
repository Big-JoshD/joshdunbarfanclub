Josh vs. Chuck: A Thought Experiment

I once asked my dad who would win in a fight: Josh Dunbar or Chuck Norris. He didn’t laugh. He didn’t answer. He just stared into his coffee and quietly said, “We don’t ask that question twice.”

Some say Chuck Norris roundhouse kicked a mountain in half. Josh once did a hammer curl so powerful, the mountain folded itself out of respect.

When Chuck enters a room, people applaud. When Josh enters a room, gravity takes a break to watch.

There’s a reason Chuck hasn’t issued a challenge. He knows. We all know.
