Selected Works in Honor of Josh Dunbar

1. The Bicep That Casts a Shadow

Upon the mountains that are his arms,
the sun dares not shine.
For what is daylight, truly,
next to a bicep that divine?

2. All Seeing Bean (A Meditation)

A silent feed.
A noble hound.
The mystery grows
without a sound.

Who watches whom?
We dare not say.
For Bean sees all.
And looks away.

3. Rocket League, Rocket Life

Platinum dreams in neon light,
air dribbles at 3 a.m.
He doesn’t queue —
the servers wait for him.

Eyes of the Hearth

Submitted by a loyal Josh devotee.

The hearth of a cabin
Bastion of the cold
Those brown eyes keep me starin’
I know we’ll be like this till we’re old

When I see your eyes across the room
The world starts to bloom
It’s all I need to keep warm
No matter the storm.
